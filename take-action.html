How You Can Help

Your participation can make a huge difference in the fight against poverty. Whether you choose to donate, volunteer, or simply spread the word, every effort counts.

Local & Global Charities

Pembrokeshire Action To Combat Hardship (PATCH)

Based in Pembrokeshire, PATCH provides emergency relief to families in financial crisis. They focus on offering practical support—such as food and essential household items—to help people get through tough times.

Visit: patchcharity.org.uk

Shelter Cymru

This charity works exclusively in Wales, providing advice, advocacy, and legal support to tackle housing issues and homelessness—a key aspect of poverty. Their efforts help ensure that vulnerable people have access to secure and decent homes.

Visit: sheltercymru.org.uk

Oxfam International

A globally recognized charity, Oxfam works around the world to end poverty and injustice. They combine emergency relief with long‐term development and campaigning efforts to create sustainable change.

Visit: oxfam.org/en

Ways to Get Involved

  • Donate: Contribute funds to support local and global initiatives.
  • Volunteer: Offer your time and skills to support community programs.
  • Advocate: Spread the word on social media and encourage others to join the cause.
